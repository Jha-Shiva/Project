Gulab Jamun

Ingredients for Gulab Jamun

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk (or as needed)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 4-5 cardamom pods (crushed)
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Recipe for Gulab Jamun

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and ghee.
  2. Gradually add milk and knead into a soft dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into small, smooth balls without cracks.
  4. Heat ghee or oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry the balls on low heat until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  6. In a separate pan, prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and crushed cardamom pods.
  7. Simmer the syrup for 5-7 minutes and add saffron (if desired).
  8. Soak the fried Gulab Jamun balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 30 minutes.
  9. Serve warm or at room temperature and enjoy!

Barfi

Ingredients for Barfi

  • 1 cup of milk powder
  • 1/2 cup of condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnishing

Recipe for Barfi

  1. Heat a non-stick pan and add ghee (clarified butter).
  2. Add milk powder, condensed milk, and milk to the pan and mix well.
  3. Cook the mixture on low heat while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Add sugar and cardamom powder, and keep stirring until the mixture thickens.
  5. Once the mixture starts leaving the sides of the pan, remove it from heat.
  6. Grease a tray or plate with ghee and pour the mixture evenly.
  7. Garnish with chopped nuts like almonds and pistachios.
  8. Let it cool and set for about 1-2 hours at room temperature or in the fridge.
  9. Cut into square or diamond shapes and serve.
